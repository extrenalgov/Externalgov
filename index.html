Sargis Mnatsakanyan appointed Honorary Consul of Saint Lucia

Published: April 2026

The appointment was made by the competent authorities of Saint Lucia as part of efforts to strengthen and develop bilateral relations between the Republic of Armenia and Saint Lucia.

The appointment is aimed at expanding trade and economic cooperation, developing business relations, and strengthening humanitarian and cultural interaction between the two countries.

In his capacity as Honorary Consul, Sargis Mnatsakanyan will contribute to the development of bilateral relations, support initiatives, and facilitate the establishment of new areas of cooperation.

The appointment reflects the mutual interest of the parties in strengthening partnership and expanding international engagement.

Additional information will be provided as activities are implemented.
